About Us

Dedicated to Transforming Educational Administration

Founded in 2020, Cerisee was created with a clear vision: to eliminate administrative burdens for schools, colleges, and universities, so educators can spend more time on their core mission—teaching and inspiring students.

We build intelligent, secure, and user-friendly software that unifies student profiles, attendance, fees, exams, and communication in a single scalable portal. By empowering decision-makers with real-time statistics, we help institutions operate with higher efficiency and agility.

Constant Innovation Always updating features to match modern school standards.
Unified Solution Consolidating 15+ disconnected systems into a single hub.

Our Vision

To become the global operating system for modern educational institutions, making school administration completely effortless.

Our Mission

Empowering educators with highly secure, reliable, and intelligent digital platforms that scale with their growth.
